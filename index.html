About Me

While pursuing my degree, I keep myself busy with Mines' Association for Computing Machinery Club (ACM). If I'm not squashing bugs on Mines' notorious Clue Game CSCI306 assignment, I'm on the Rugby pitch playing second row ('sko digs!). I'm interested in data science and machine learning, and I'm always looking for cool projects to contribute to. I like studying with my friends, going to the gym, and biking through the beautiful town of Golden, CO.

I first fell in love with Computer Science when I was 11. My brother came home from school one day, eager to show me and my sister "The Hour of Code". I remember being enthralled by what could be created with just a few blocks of code. A decade later, I've written thousands of lines of code, contributed to dozens of projects, and developed a deep love for tech and what it can be.
